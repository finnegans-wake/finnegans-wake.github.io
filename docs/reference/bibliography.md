# Bibliography

A curated bibliography of essential and recommended works for *Finnegans Wake* study.

## The Text

### Standard Edition
**Finnegans Wake** by James Joyce
- Viking Press / Penguin Books
- ISBN: 978-0141183114
- This pagination is used by McHugh and all scholarly references

### Alternative Editions
- **Faber and Faber** (UK) — Same pagination
- **Oxford World's Classics** (2012) — Includes introduction by Finn Fordham

## Joseph Campbell's Joyce Works

Campbell's writing on Joyce spans his entire career, from his 1941 obituary notice to lectures delivered shortly before his death in 1987.

### Primary Works

| Title | Author(s) | Year | Description |
|-------|-----------|------|-------------|
| *A Skeleton Key to Finnegans Wake* | Joseph Campbell & Henry Morton Robinson | 1944/2013 | First comprehensive guide; Vico interpretation |
| *Mythic Worlds, Modern Words: On the Art of James Joyce* | Joseph Campbell (ed. Edmund L. Epstein) | 2004 | Collected essays and lectures |
| *The Mythic Novels of James Joyce* | Joseph Campbell | 1983 | Esalen Institute lectures (7+ hours audio) |

## Building Your Library

### Minimum (Free)
1. Text (library copy or public domain where available)
2. [FWEET](http://fweet.org) bookmarked

### Recommended ($50-100)
3. Viking/Penguin *Finnegans Wake*
4. McHugh's *Annotations* (used copies available)
5. Campbell's *Skeleton Key*

### Complete ($200-300)
6. Glasheen's *Third Census*
7. Tindall's *Reader's Guide*
8. Hart's *Concordance*
9. Bishop's *Joyce's Book of the Dark*
10. Ellmann's *James Joyce* biography
